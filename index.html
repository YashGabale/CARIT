Welcome to CARIT

The creation and implementation of interventions aimed at enhancing the standard of living for people as individuals, families, and communities are made possible by the Center for Applied Research in Human Development. Our specific goal is to enhance the quality
of life for marginalized sociodemographic groups by assessing and guiding public benefit actions.

Center supports community- and state-based organizations in creating, implementing, and assessing human services initiatives. The goal
of this partnership with local and state agencies is to make it easier to create excellent program evaluations.

In addition, the Center provides graduate students with the chance to gain knowledge of the research and publication process from professionals in the fields of family science, applied research, and human development.
